Omelette

Description

A timeless classic: Feel free to add the filling of your choice to this lunch or snack of egg.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a non-stick frying pan over a medium-low heat until the butter melts and is foaming.
  2. Pour the eggs into the pan, tilting the pan slightly to allow the eggs to swirl and cover the pan completely.
  3. Let the mixture cook for approximately 20 seconds then scrape a line through the middle with a spatula.
  4. Tilt the pan again to allow it to fill back up with the runny egg. Repeat once or twice more until the egg has just set.
  5. Scatter any desired filling over the top of the omelette and fold gently in half with the spatula.

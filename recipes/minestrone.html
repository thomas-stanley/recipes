Minestrone

Description

A hearty bowl of soup: Can be whipped up in 10 minutes so is perfect for a quick lunch or warming supper.

Ingredients

  • 1l hot vegetable stock
  • 400g tin chopped tomato
  • 100g thin spaghetti, broken into short lengths
  • 350g frozen mixed vegetables
  • 4tbsp pesto
  • A drizzle of olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese, to serve

Steps

  1. Bring the stock to boil with the tomatoes, then add the spaghetti.
  2. Cook for 6 minutes, or until done.
  3. A few minutes before the spaghetti is ready, add the vegetables and bring back to boil.
  4. Simmer for 2 minutes until everything is cooked
  5. Add olive oil, pesto, and parmesan cheese to the finished soup for additional flavour.

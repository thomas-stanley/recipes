Miso Soup

Description

The perfect starter or snack: A miso soup with soft cubes of tofu, crunchy curls of seeweed, and aromatic spring onions.

Ingredients

  • 5g dried wakame seeweed
  • 1l dashi
  • 200g fresh silken tofu, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 2tbsp white miso paste
  • 3tbsp red miso paste
  • Finely chopped spring onion, to serve

Steps

  1. Put the wakame in a small bowl and cover with cold water, then leave it for 5 mins until the leaves have fully expanded.
  2. Make the dashi or heat until it reaches a rolling boil. Add the tofu and cook for 1 min before adding the seeweed.
  3. Reduce the heat. Put both types of miso in a ladle and dip it into the pot. Slowly loosen up the miso with a spoon inside the ladle .
  4. Once all the miso is dissolved into the soup, turn off the heat immediately. Sprinkle with chopped spring onions to add colour and fragrance.
